# Stellar Dominion — Technology List

Technologies are defined in `techs.json`. Trees: **Physical** (0), **Social** (1), **Xenological** (2).

---

## Physical Sciences

### Tier 1

#### Automated Mining

Replaces manual extraction crews with semi-autonomous drilling rigs coordinated by basic logistics AI, enabling extraction under conditions fatal to organic labour.


#### Deep Survey Protocols

Standardises sensor packages and logging procedures across all science vessels, allowing systematic cataloguing of stellar bodies, subsurface deposits, and gravitational anomalies.


#### Orbital Fabrication

Establishes zero-gravity orbital platforms where alloys can be cast and hulls assembled without surface-to-orbit transport costs.


#### Propellant Chemistry

Basic high-efficiency propellant formulations that extend early fleet range beyond chemical rockets and reduce fuel consumption on short hauls.


### Tier 2

#### Advanced Metallurgy

Unlocks high-pressure alloy synthesis, heat treatment protocols, and the first composite hull materials. Cruiser-class hulls become feasible.


#### Fusion Drive Arrays

Replaces fission propulsion with compact fusion drives; gas consumption becomes the primary fuel constraint and Ice extraction becomes economically critical.


#### Gravitational Scanning

Develops gravitometric sensors sensitive enough to detect buried ore concentrations, subsurface voids, and the signatures of exotic deposits not visible to optical or chemical survey.


#### Orbital Mechanics Mastery

Formal mathematical models of multi-body gravitational systems, enabling precise navigation, orbit insertion, and fuel-optimal intercept trajectories.


#### Plasma Containment Fundamentals

Magnetic bottle designs capable of sustaining plasma at temperatures relevant to both power generation and directed energy weapons. The first practical precursor to beam armaments.


### Tier 3

#### Exotic Mineral Survey

Enhanced long-range detection algorithms tuned to the gravitational and electromagnetic signatures of strategic resource deposits. Reduces the galaxy survey time to find Void Gas, Nanite Dust, and Dark Lattice.


#### Gravitational Engineering

Extends scanning into active manipulation — localised gravitational field generators strong enough to assist extraction, reinforce structural materials, and guide plasma flows in reactor cores.


#### High-Energy Particle Physics

Studies particles accelerated to relativistic velocities and the exotic interactions they produce — the theoretical basis for particle beam weapons and later for understanding dark matter.


#### Star-Lifting Theory

Develops the theoretical framework for extracting matter directly from stellar atmospheres using magnetically-focused solar wind amplification. No practical star-lifting yet — this is the science that proves it can be done.


#### Stellite Extraction

Ancient crystalline formations found in only 3–6 systems per galaxy. This research provides the containment and cutting protocols to harvest them without triggering the resonant fracture cascade that destroys unshielded equipment.


### Tier 4

#### Dark Energy Theory

Formalises interactions between Void Gas, Dark Lattice, and conventional matter under extreme field conditions. Void Gas Condensers become buildable. The gateway to Spacetime Engineering.


#### Degnite Extraction

Electron-degenerate matter found only in neutron star systems — impossible to synthesise. Develops the magnetic confinement harnesses required to extract, transport, and work with it without catastrophic release.


#### Magnetic Confinement Mastery

Advances magnetic field shaping from reactor-scale to structural-scale applications, dramatically reducing the risk profile of Degnite work and improving power generation density.


#### Relativistic Drive Theory

Formal treatment of propulsion systems approaching meaningful fractions of lightspeed, and the spacetime distortion effects that produce apparent FTL travel. The theoretical backbone of the jump drive.


#### Spacetime Engineering

Develops practical application of spacetime curvature manipulation. The Quantum Smelter improves alloy ratios via micro-compression fields. Jump drives become feasible.


### Tier 5

#### Aetheric Lattice Synthesis

Synthesises Stellite, Void Gas, and Nanite Dust simultaneously into a self-repairing structural material with tensile strength near the strong nuclear force. Every megastructure depends on it.


#### Gravitational Press

The Birch World bootstrap problem: Degnite must be worked under gravitational compression that destroys ordinary machinery — so the Press itself must be built from Degnite first. This research unlocks the multi-stage quest chain that solves that paradox.


#### Quantum Crystallography

Studies the lattice structure of Dark Lattice formations at the quantum level, revealing the computational potential that Computronium Theory (Xeno T4) will eventually exploit.


#### Stellar Plasma Engineering

Moves star-lifting from theory to practice at small scale — controlled extraction of stellar plasma jets for direct material harvesting. Lays the groundwork for the industrial star-lifting required by megastructures.


#### Voidweave Fabrication

A micro-wormhole lattice that distributes mechanical stress across folded space. Required for Ringworld stress layers, Dyson collectors, and Alderson edge stabilisation.


### Tier 6

#### Gravitational Lens Arrays

System-scale gravitational focusing arrays that can direct solar radiation, accelerate particles, or create localised high-gravity zones for exotic material synthesis. The first step toward treating a star system as an engineering platform.


#### Neutronium Alloys

Working with near-degenerate matter at densities beyond conventional metallurgy produces hull materials of extraordinary strength-to-mass ratio. Requires the Gravitational Press to work at all.


#### Photon Capture Grid

Preliminary orbital collector arrays that intercept and convert a fraction of stellar output to energy. A prototype of the Dyson Sphere concept — incomplete but immediately useful.


#### Resonant Field Architecture

Precise tuning of Aetheric Lattice resonance frequencies reduces the probability of the cascade failures that arise from Nanite Dust fluctuations. The engineering discipline that makes megastructures survivable long-term.


#### Void Drive Arrays

Integrates Voidweave micro-wormhole technology into drive systems — fleet transit via temporarily stabilised spacetime shortcuts rather than conventional propulsion.


### Tier 7

#### Dark Matter Catalysis

Practical harnessing of dark matter particles as a reactor fuel — not combustion but catalytic annihilation. Energy output per unit volume surpasses anything achievable with conventional fusion.


#### Dimensional Stress Analysis

Formal engineering discipline for calculating how megastructure-scale objects interact with the curvature of spacetime, predicting stress concentrations that conventional structural analysis cannot detect.


#### Neutron Star Engineering

Exploits the extreme conditions of neutron star systems beyond simple Degnite extraction — tidal power harvesting, exotic particle extraction, and proximity construction using the star's own magnetic field as a tool.


#### Quantum Tunneling Fabrication

Controlled quantum tunneling as a manufacturing technique — particles placed through barriers rather than moved around them. Allows fabrication tolerances impossible by any macroscale method.


#### Tachyon Detection Arrays

Sensor systems sensitive to the tachyon wake produced by anything travelling faster than light. Provides strategic early warning of incoming fleets and jump-capable vessels.


### Tier 8

#### Dark Matter Engineering

Moves dark matter from fuel source to construction material — shaped dark matter structures that interact with gravity but not electromagnetism, enabling entirely new classes of building and weapon.


#### Gravitational Siege Weapons

Weapons that weaponise gravitational tidal forces — fleet-mounted tidal disruptors, planetary bombardment systems that induce tectonic stress, and siege platforms that can collapse orbital infrastructure.


#### Planetary Deconstruction Protocols

The practical engineering doctrine for efficiently dismantling planets for raw mass — sequencing, mass transport, and hazard management for operations that kill a world in years rather than millennia.


#### Point Singularity Warheads

Micro-singularities with a survival time of milliseconds — sufficient to produce a gravitational pulse capable of tearing hulls, collapsing shields, and permanently disabling critical infrastructure.


#### Stellar Architecture Theory

Formal engineering discipline treating a star system as a single construction object — stress analysis at AU scale, material flow modelling across stellar distances, and construction sequencing for projects measured in decades.


#### Unified Field Theory

A single mathematical framework reconciling gravity, electromagnetism, and the strong and weak nuclear forces. The theoretical foundation without which T10+ physical research is impossible. A cross-branch gate: also requires Post-Biological Cognition (Xeno T9 Shared) or Digital Society Transition (Soc T9 Shared).


### Tier 9

#### Binary Star Engineering

Exploiting multi-star systems as coordinated energy and mass sources — orbital mechanics tuned for optimal energy capture, mass transfer between stellar companions, and exotic particle harvesting from stellar interactions.


#### Dimensional Siege Arrays

Weapons systems that exploit spacetime geometry to bypass conventional shielding — attacks routed through pocket-space, emerging inside target hulls. The most tactically decisive weapons before T12.


#### Hyperspace Engineering

Stable, persistent hyperspace lane construction rather than one-time jumps — infrastructure that creates permanent transit corridors between systems. Changes fleet logistics fundamentally.


#### Nova Bomb Theory

A weapon that initiates uncontrolled stellar fusion amplification — in theory, capable of inducing a nova in a target star system. Possession alone is a galactic diplomatic event. Use is another.


#### Quantum Foam Manipulation

Working at Planck scale — manipulating the quantum foam that constitutes the underlying substrate of spacetime. The bridge between conventional physics and the reality-engineering research that follows.


#### Stellar Mass Redistribution

Industrial-scale star-lifting — extracting stellar mass at a rate relevant to megastructure construction timelines. Moving billions of tonnes of stellar material per year becomes routine engineering.


### Tier 10

#### Advanced Stellar Engineering

The synthesis of weapons physics and megastructure physics — understanding that the same forces that tear hulls apart can hold a Ringworld together, if controlled precisely enough.


#### Dark Energy Channeling

Directing large-scale dark energy flows through artificially constructed channels — not merely harvesting dark energy passively but actively routing it like electricity through wire.


#### Matter-Energy Equivalence Engines

Direct matter-to-energy conversion at industrial scale — not nuclear reactions but mass annihilation at efficiencies approaching Einstein's limit. The energy available from a single asteroid exceeds a star's lifetime output.


#### Null Space Theory

Understanding the physics between dimensional membranes — not empty space but a structured medium with its own rules. The map of territory that future expansion will cross.


#### Spacetime Fabric Weaving

Stable artificial spacetime constructs — geometric pockets with custom curvature, dimensions not naturally present in this universe, and topology that allows for storage and transit impossible in normal space.


### Tier 11

#### Dimensional Membrane Theory

A complete formal model of the dimensional structure of reality — how many layers exist, how thick the membranes between them are, and under what conditions they can be crossed.


#### Event Horizon Engineering

Controlled manipulation of black hole event horizons for construction and power — adjusting the Hawking radiation profile, stabilising the ergosphere for near-horizon operations, and anchoring megastructures in extreme gravity.


#### Quantum Vacuum Engineering

Extracting energy from the quantum vacuum — the baseline energy state of empty space. Theoretically limitless; practically constrained by the difficulty of coupling to vacuum fluctuations at exploitable scale.


#### Spacetime Topology Mastery

Reshaping the geometric properties of local space — changing the number of spatial dimensions in a volume, creating non-orientable regions, and designing spatial configurations that have no natural analogue.


#### Stellar Core Intervention

Direct manipulation of stellar fusion processes — adjusting hydrogen consumption rates, changing spectral output, extending stellar lifespans, or triggering controlled burnoff. A star under control rather than simply orbited.


### Tier 12

#### Birch World Architecture

A habitable shell enclosing a black hole or neutron star. Gravity points outward. Hawking radiation shielding incorporated into the Aetheric Lattice surface layer.


#### Hawking Radiation Harnessing

Amplifying and collecting the Hawking radiation emitted by black holes — typically negligibly small, but engineerable at stellar-mass scales into a significant power source with appropriate focusing infrastructure.


#### Ringworld Engineering

The accumulated synthesis of everything Physical Sciences has developed. Requires disassembling all planets in the target system for raw mass.


#### Stellar Forge

Complete reconstruction of star systems — rebuilding planets from stored mass reserves, moving stellar bodies into new configurations, and constructing entirely artificial stellar objects.


#### Void Anchor Network

Permanent, stable wormhole infrastructure rather than temporary jump corridors — anchored to dark matter frames that prevent the topology from collapsing. The backbone of a truly galactic empire.


### Tier 13

#### Causality Engineering

Local manipulation of cause-effect relationships — creating temporal structures where effects precede causes, events loop stably, or causal chains are deliberately severed. Applied to construction, this means building things before the resources to build them exist.


#### Dark Flow Channeling

Harnessing the large-scale dark energy currents that flow through the universe at cosmic scales — not local dark energy but the directional flows that influence galaxy cluster motion.


#### Metric Engineering

Reshaping spacetime geometry at will within defined volumes — creating local regions where the speed of light, gravitational constant, or dimensional count differ from the broader universe.


#### Physical Constants Research

Understanding why the universe's constants have their specific values — whether they are the only possible values, or one choice among infinite alternatives. The first steps toward changing them.


#### Pocket Dimension Seeding

Creating stable micro-dimensional spaces — not just pockets of normal space but regions with custom topology, separate conservation laws, and entrance points that can be placed anywhere.


### Tier 14

#### Bubble Universe Theory

Formal framework for spawning self-consistent physical domains — regions with their own conservation laws, physical constants, and spacetime topology that are self-sustaining rather than requiring external maintenance.


#### Causal Loop Architecture

Stable, self-consistent time loops used as a construction method — the finished structure is part of the causal chain that enables its own construction. Requires mathematical proof of consistency before construction begins.


#### Dimensional Membrane Thinning

Reducing the energetic barrier between dimensional layers — making it easier to access null space, cross between dimensional membranes, and eventually construct bridges to entirely separate realities.


#### Fundamental Force Modulation

Adjusting the effective strength of electromagnetism and gravity within controlled volumes — weakening gravity to allow construction methods otherwise impossible, or strengthening electromagnetism to create materials with extraordinary properties.


### Tier 15

#### Eternal Compression Drive

An escape from the heat death of the universe — a propulsion system that uses dark flow currents to accelerate the civilisation toward regions of higher entropy gradient, extending habitable existence indefinitely. The empire does not transcend physics. It outruns its end.


#### Pocket Reality Engine

A self-sustaining new universe with designed physical laws — not a simulation but a genuine physical domain separate from this one, accessible from this universe via a permanent bridge. The empire can migrate civilisation here.


#### The Physical Law Codex

A complete written description of this universe's fundamental rules — every physical law, constant, and relationship expressed in a single unified formalism. The knowledge required to write a different universe.



## Social Sciences

### Tier 1

#### Census and Logistics

Population tracking systems and resource flow modelling that allow the empire to predict shortages before they become cascades — the first warning layer of the cascade prevention system.


#### Colonial Administration

Establishes bureaucratic infrastructure for managing populations across multiple worlds — census systems, resource allocation protocols, and the first administrative hierarchy.


#### Democratic Councils

Formal deliberative structures that give population factions representation in governance decisions, reducing unrest and improving Influence generation by legitimising authority.


#### Trade Network Protocols

Defines the legal, logistical, and communication standards that allow trade to flow between systems. The Trade Hub becomes constructable.


### Tier 2

#### Bureaucratic Reform

Streamlines administrative processes, reduces redundancy, and introduces specialised governance roles. Admin efficiency improves empire-wide.


#### Economic Modeling

Formal economic prediction algorithms trained on multi-system resource flow data. Reduces the frequency of resource cascade events by giving the empire earlier warning of developing shortages.


#### Media and Propaganda Networks

Information control and distribution infrastructure — state media, cultural programming, and the engineering of consensus. Unity output increases. Public opinion becomes a managed resource.


#### Megaproject Doctrine

The governance frameworks required to mobilise an entire empire's industrial capacity toward a single construction goal. No megastructure is legally or logistically possible without this.


### Tier 3

#### Advanced Pop Management

Large-scale demographic management — growth incentives, resource-to-happiness modelling, and the engineering required to keep enormous populations stable. Arcology Spires become constructable.


#### Interstellar Law Framework

Formal legal frameworks governing relations between star systems and empires — contracts, treaties, and the mechanisms for enforcing them across distances where communication takes months.


#### Social Engineering

Targeted modification of population cultural traits and ethical distributions — not passive propaganda but active reshaping of who a population believes itself to be.


#### System Disassembly Mandate

The legal and moral framework that permits an empire to legislate the complete disassembly of a star system's planetary bodies for construction material. Entire worlds become building supplies.


### Tier 4

#### Cultural Homogenization

Reduces inter-planetary cultural friction through propaganda standardisation, education reform, and cultural exchange. Unity generation increases. Diversity decreases.


#### Galactic Market Integration

Integrates the empire's trade infrastructure into a galaxy-wide commodity exchange with dynamic pricing. Resource surpluses that would otherwise stagnate can be liquidated.


#### Imperial Doctrine

Military governance improvements — occupation efficiency, conquered world integration speed, and the legal authority to deploy military force inside the empire's own borders.


#### Species Integration Protocols

Management frameworks for multi-species empires — converting alien pops from a source of instability into functional contributors by addressing the specific cultural friction points of each species combination.


### Tier 5

#### Galactic Citizenship Act

Trans-species governance framework that defines rights and responsibilities applying to all sapient life regardless of species, reducing the diplomatic friction of multi-species diplomacy.


#### Post-Scarcity Diplomacy

An empire that has mastered resource abundance approaches diplomacy from a position of genuine generosity — and genuine leverage. Consumer Goods no longer require Ore input at T5.


#### Stellar Engineering Corps

A permanent, empire-wide professional corps of megastructure engineers, stellar architects, and construction logistics specialists.


#### Utopian Urban Planning

Planetary development optimisation — integrated Housing, Amenities, and production districts designed as systems rather than individual buildings. Synergy bonuses between adjacent building types.


### Tier 6

#### Cognitive Liberty Acts

Legal frameworks protecting the right of sapient minds to remain unmodified — a direct reaction to Social Engineering. Defines the boundary between permissible cultural influence and prohibited cognitive manipulation.


#### Interstellar Federation Law

Multi-empire legal frameworks that allow alliance mechanics to function formally — binding agreements, arbitration bodies, and the shared legal infrastructure that makes sustained cooperation possible.


#### Post-Scarcity Theory

Academic research into what a post-scarcity civilisation actually looks like — the social structures, governance models, and cultural forms that emerge when material want is eliminated. Foundation for T7+ Social research.


#### Resource Sharing Protocols

Formal cross-empire resource exchange frameworks that reduce the value of blockades and resource-denial strategies. An empire with Resource Sharing Protocols converts scarcity from a weapon into a diplomacy tool.


#### The Great Optimization

Empire-wide efficiency review — a once-per-game social technology that generates a massive one-time bonus to all resource outputs as systematic inefficiencies are identified and corrected.


### Tier 7

#### Cultural Propagation Networks

Active cultural spread to neighbouring systems and empires — not passive cultural influence but designed cultural exports that shift the ethics distributions of populations beyond the empire's borders.


#### Diplomatic Immunity Frameworks

Protects ambassadors and diplomatic personnel even in wartime, improving espionage countermeasures and reducing the risk that diplomatic incidents escalate to conflict.


#### Galactic Heritage Preservation

Legal and logistical protection for precursor archaeological sites, preventing their destruction during system disassembly or megastructure construction. Generates ongoing Xenological Research.


#### Synthetic Governance

AI administrative assistance — not replacing sapient leadership but augmenting it with systems that track millions of decisions simultaneously and surface conflicts and inefficiencies humans miss.


#### The Population Singularity Theory

Modelling the behaviour of civilisations at billion-pop scale — emergent behaviours, phase transitions in social organisation, and the governance challenges that arise when a population is too large for any individual to comprehend.


### Tier 8

#### Cultural Erasure Programs

Forcible reshaping of conquered population ethics and cultural traits — the technology of making a people forget who they were and become who you need them to be.


#### Galactic Governance Reforms

Fundamental restructuring of council mechanics — new positions become available, legitimacy thresholds shift, and the agenda system is expanded. Two additional council positions unlock.


#### Multi-Species Parliament

Governance structures where alien species are not just tolerated but are structural participants in imperial government — turning multi-species composition from a stability challenge into a strategic asset.


#### Planetary Subjugation Doctrine

Rapid-integration doctrine for conquered worlds — occupation systems, population resettlement protocols, and administrative machinery that converts a conquered planet into a productive asset within years rather than decades.


#### Post-Biological Ethics

Legal frameworks extending rights to synthetic, uploaded, and post-biological sapients — defining personhood in terms of cognition rather than substrate. Required for governing empires that have begun digital ascension.


#### Voluntary Integration Framework

Peaceful annexation mechanics — legal and cultural infrastructure that allows neighbouring populations to voluntarily join the empire, receiving full citizenship in exchange.


### Tier 9

#### Digital Society Transition

Formal governance structures for societies where a significant fraction of the population exists digitally — managing coexistence, resource allocation, and political representation between biological and digital populations.


#### Galactic Commons Charter

Shared resource zones that reduce economic warfare effectiveness and create mutual dependency between empires — making war economically irrational for all parties with access to the Commons.


#### Posthuman Rights Framework

Legal recognition of post-biological, digital, and psionic citizens as full political participants — the infrastructure for governing empires where the majority of the population may not be biologically human in any meaningful sense.


#### Star System Annexation Law

Legal machinery for rapid territorial expansion following military victory — binding political integration of entire star systems within months rather than decades.


#### Total War Mobilisation Doctrine

Empire-wide wartime economic mode — conscription protocols, production priority reallocation, and governance suspension mechanisms that shift maximum industrial output to military production.


#### Unified Species Council

Pan-species governance body representing all sapient life within the empire as a unified political entity — the final expression of Synthesis philosophy.


### Tier 10

#### Advanced Galactic Governance

The synthesis of Dominion and Synthesis paths — understanding that the same governance infrastructure that enables rapid conquest also enables voluntary integration, depending on use.


#### Consciousness Economics

Economic models designed for populations where the boundary between worker, product, and consumer is blurred — digital minds that can copy themselves, modify their own labour capacity, and generate cultural output as a byproduct of existence.


#### Cultural Substrate Encoding

Embedding cultural values directly into planetary infrastructure — architecture, information systems, and environmental design that continuously reinforces cultural identity passively.


#### Distributed Governance Networks

Decentralised administration immune to capital destruction — governance functions distributed across hundreds of nodes so that losing any single system cannot collapse imperial authority.


#### Empire-Wide Neural Mesh

Communication network connecting all population centers simultaneously — not just faster communication but a new kind of collective awareness across the empire that changes the nature of governance itself.


### Tier 11

#### Consciousness Rights Framework

Legal structures for uploaded, distributed, and networked minds — defining property rights, criminal liability, political representation, and the right to copy oneself.


#### Dyson Sphere Program

Organises the multi-decade empire-wide programme required to construct a Dyson Sphere: political will, labour allocation, material stockpiling, and construction phasing. The social side of what is also an engineering challenge.


#### Post-Scarcity Civilisation Standards

Formal definition of the post-scarcity state — the social, legal, and economic benchmarks that identify when material want has been eliminated as a driver of behaviour. Massive happiness bonus empire-wide.


#### The Final Economy

The theoretical endpoint of economic growth — a model of civilisation where the concept of resource allocation becomes meaningless because every resource exists in abundance.


#### Transcendent Culture Protocols

Cultural practices that persist beyond biological death — the transmission of identity, memory, and values across generations of post-biological citizens and into digital substrate.


### Tier 12

#### Alderson Disk Architecture

A flat disc spanning an entire star system — structurally more stable than the Ringworld because there is no rotational stress. Edge stabilisation via Voidweave is the primary engineering challenge.


#### Post-Material Governance

Administrative structures for populations that have transcended physical needs — governing minds for whom material allocation is irrelevant and the only resources that matter are computation, experience, and meaning.


#### Ring Civilisation Doctrine

Governance systems designed for populations living on megastructures — administration of populations in the billions across continuous surface areas with no natural borders.


#### The Eternal Administration

Administrative frameworks that function across geological time — institutional structures designed to persist for millennia without human maintenance, encoding governance principles into the empire's physical infrastructure.


### Tier 13

#### Collective Consciousness Governance

Governing merged or networked minds as unified political entities — the point where the boundary between individual, community, and government begins to dissolve.


#### Galactic Mind-State Protocols

Standards for consciousness transfer and storage compatible with other empires — enabling population exchange, mind-state diplomacy, and the sharing of cultural experiences directly between civilisations.


#### Post-Individual Ethics

Moral frameworks where the individual is no longer the unit of value — ethics designed for beings whose identities overlap, merge, and split fluidly.


#### Reality Consent Frameworks

Legal structures for situations where one entity can alter another's reality — the governance of reality engineering. Defines consent, sovereignty, and harm in a universe where physics can be locally rewritten.


#### Temporal Governance Theory

Administrative theory for populations that experience time differently — digital minds running at different clock speeds, entities whose causal experience is non-linear, and governance structures that must remain coherent across temporal distortions.


### Tier 14

#### Causality Committee

A governing body with formal authority over timeline integrity — adjudicating disputes arising from time loop construction, causal manipulation, and the accidental creation of paradoxes.


#### Multi-Reality Governance

Administering populations spread across dimensional spaces — governing an empire where some citizens live in pocket universes with different physical laws.


#### The Post-Physical Social Contract

Governance for beings who have left physical existence — a political theory for populations that exist as pure information, nested simulations, or distributed consciousness in non-physical substrate.


#### Universal Civilisation Standard

A proposed standard for all sapient life in the galaxy — a common framework of rights, capabilities, and responsibilities that transcends species, substrate, and dimensional location. Adoption is voluntary. The consequences of non-adoption are not.


### Tier 15

#### Post-Reality Social Order

Governance structures for a civilisation that has moved beyond physics — social organisation that persists after physical transcendence, managing the transition so that nothing meaningful is lost.


#### Stellar Consciousness

The empire itself becomes a distributed galactic intelligence — not a collection of individuals governed by shared institutions but a single mind expressed through billions of bodies, buildings, and systems simultaneously.


#### The Final Accord

A binding agreement between all surviving civilisations on the nature of reality — a shared constitutional framework for a universe in which multiple civilisations can rewrite physical laws. The alternative is war over the definition of existence.



## Xenological Sciences

### Tier 1

#### Alien Biology

Establishes comparative xenobiology as a discipline — cataloguing the biochemical, genetic, and ecological variation found across alien species.


#### Precursor Archaeology

Systematic study of ancient civilisations — excavation doctrine, artefact preservation, and the comparative analysis methods that allow precursor technology to be understood rather than merely found.


#### Symbiotic Studies

Cross-species cooperation research — the biochemical and social dynamics of organisms that evolved in mutual dependence, with direct applications to multi-species population happiness.


#### Xenochemistry

Studies exotic chemical processes in alien organisms and non-standard planetary environments — silicon-based metabolisms, plasma-adapted biochemistry, the resonant properties of crystalline life.


### Tier 2

#### Genetic Engineering

Develops the tools to deliberately modify genetic sequences in both agricultural organisms and sapient populations. Biomass yields increase dramatically.


#### Nanite Fundamentals

Nanite Dust cannot be safely used without understanding the emergent behaviours that arise in nanite colonies. Establishes containment protocols and the first practical applications.


#### Pathogen Research

Disease modelling, biological weapon defense, and pharmaceutical synthesis improvements — understanding what can kill a population is foundational to preventing it.


#### Xenolinguistics

Alien communication protocols — pattern recognition systems for non-standard sapient communication, with direct applications to first contact outcomes and precursor language decoding.


### Tier 3

#### Nanite Integration

Moves beyond containment into active integration — programming nanite colonies for engineering tasks, embedding them in hull materials for self-repair, incorporating them into pharmaceutical synthesis.


#### Neural Interface Protocols

Mind-machine interface systems — direct neural connections to digital systems, with immediate applications to research output and later to digital consciousness transfer.


#### Psionic Research

Documents and systematises psionic phenomena in certain species — resonant mental fields, probability distortion, the anomalous properties of Psionic Ore.


#### Xenogenetic Cataloguing

Comprehensive alien genetic database — documenting the full diversity of known species' genomes, enabling genetic engineering protocols that work on non-standard biologies.


### Tier 4

#### Biosphere Engineering

Planet-wide ecosystem design — the engineering of stable, self-sustaining biospheres from scratch, with tailored species relationships and resource cycles.


#### Cognitive Enhancement

Deliberate improvement of organic sapient intelligence — genetic modifications, neural augmentations, and environmental design that measurably increase cognitive capacity.


#### Computronium Theory

Develops the theoretical framework for Computronium — matter restructured at the quantum level to perform computational operations as densely as physics permits.


#### Digital Consciousness Theory

The groundwork for mind uploading — formal models of consciousness as an information process that can, in principle, run on any sufficiently complex substrate.


#### Psionic Ore Applications

Translates psionic theory into engineering practice. Psionic Ore's resonant properties become load-bearing in two late-game material chains. Required for Voidweave.


### Tier 5

#### Consciousness Cartography

Mapping the structure of sapient mind-space — identifying the common architectural features across different species' consciousness, revealing that all sapience shares deep structural similarities.


#### Inner-Shell Computronium

Fabrication protocols for the Inner Shell of a Computronium substrate — the densest computational layer, primary in the energy-capture hierarchy.


#### Outer-Shell Computronium

Fabrication protocols for Mid and Outer Shells. After 50 continuous months of operation, the Outer Shell's quantum matrices self-organise and Cognite begins crystallising.


#### Psionic Field Engineering

Active psionic force fields — engineering applications of concentrated psionic phenomena, including structural reinforcement, shielding, and the psionic sensor arrays that can detect consciousness at range.


#### Synthetic Biology

Creating wholly designed organisms from the ground up — not modifying existing life but authoring it. Immediate applications to agriculture, medicine, and military units.


### Tier 6

#### Biological Transcendence Theory

Theoretical framework for surpassing biological limits — not modifying existing biology but replacing it with something that fulfils the same function while being free of its inherent constraints.


#### Directed Evolution Protocols

Guided speciation on demand — controlling the evolutionary trajectory of a population over multiple generations to achieve designed traits. The pop trait modification system becomes fully available.


#### Neural Mapping Complete

Full connectome documentation of multiple sapient species — every neural connection mapped to sufficient resolution that consciousness can be modelled, and in principle, reproduced.


#### The Living Planet Hypothesis

Treating planetary biospheres as single organisms — the observation that sufficiently complex ecosystems exhibit organism-like homeostasis, adaptation, and even something resembling cognition.


#### Xenological Unification Studies

Finding the common patterns across all known species — structural, genetic, cognitive, and cultural universals that suggest either a common origin or convergent necessity.


### Tier 7

#### Collective Intelligence Theory

Understanding emergent intelligence from networked minds — the discovery that connected minds don't simply add their intelligence but multiply it in ways that produce qualitatively different capabilities.


#### Digital Mind Architecture

Formal engineering standards for synthetic consciousness — the equivalent of building codes, but for minds. Defines what counts as a successful digital mind upload and what quality standards apply.


#### Engineered Sapience

Designing new sapient species to specification — not modifying existing species but authoring sapience itself from first principles. The ultimate expression of Xenological Sciences.


#### Post-Biological Ecology

Ecosystems where digital and biological life coexist as functional components — stable ecologies that include non-biological information-processing entities as structural participants alongside organic life.


#### Psionic Amplification Networks

Planet-scale psionic field generators that amplify individual psionic ability and enable collective psionic phenomena — coordinated psionic effects that no individual could achieve alone.


### Tier 8

#### Gene-Ascendant Architecture

Biological structures grown rather than built — living megastructure precursors, engineered organisms that perform infrastructure functions at scales normally requiring manufactured materials.


#### Mass Mind Uploading

Civilisation-scale consciousness transfer to digital substrate — not individual uploading but the logistics, ethics, and technology of moving millions of minds simultaneously.


#### Psionic Reality Interface

Psionics as a means of directly interacting with physical reality — concentrated collective psionic fields that can influence quantum probability, alter local physics, and interact with the Voidweave micro-wormhole lattice.


#### Synthetic Consciousness Standards

Quality and rights standards for digital minds — ensuring that uploaded consciousness retains continuity, that synthetic minds are not merely simulations, and that all digital sapients have equal legal standing.


#### Transhuman Biology

Radical modification of organic life beyond recognisable species boundaries — organisms that retain biological substrate but are no longer constrained by evolved biological limitations.


#### Unified Consciousness Theory

Formal model unifying biological, psionic, and digital cognition — the discovery that all three are implementations of the same underlying process, which has profound implications for both engineering and ethics.


### Tier 9

#### Distributed Digital Civilisation

A population that exists entirely as distributed computation — no fixed physical bodies, no geographic constraints, no resource requirements beyond processing power and energy.


#### Gaia-Scale Organisms

Continent-spanning single organisms that function as infrastructure — organisms whose biology IS the planet's support system, replacing manufactured buildings with living equivalents at planetary scale.


#### Living Megastructure Theory

Megastructures grown from engineered organisms rather than built from manufactured materials — structural elements that self-repair, adapt, and respond to environmental stress biologically.


#### Post-Biological Cognition

Intelligence that has moved beyond the constraints of any substrate — the formal understanding that cognition is a pattern that can be instantiated in matter, energy, spacetime, or combinations not yet discovered.


#### The Sapience Threshold

Understanding the minimum and maximum possible forms of intelligence — the lower bound below which something is not sapient and the upper bound beyond which individual identity dissolves into something else.


#### Virtual Reality Substrate

Self-contained digital universes for population habitation — virtual spaces with their own physics, geography, and social structures, running on Computronium substrate.


### Tier 10

#### Consciousness Substrate Engineering

Designing optimal matter configurations for thought — not Computronium specifically but any arrangement of matter that maximises cognitive capacity per unit of mass and energy.


#### Noosphere Architecture
