Background music for the game.

Place your music files (.ogg or .mp3) in this folder or in any subfolder.
The music player will discover them and play through the list.

Example subfolders you can use:
  ambient/   - calm or atmospheric tracks
  menu/      - main menu or lobby music
  combat/    - battle or tension tracks

Files are included in playlist order by path name. Add or remove files
and they will be picked up when the game runs.
